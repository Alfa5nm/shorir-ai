# SHORIR AI Product Context

## Positioning

SHORIR AI is a browser-first movement intelligence product for beginners and returning exercisers in Bangladesh. It combines strict pose coaching, local exercise guidance, Bangladeshi diet planning, cautious calorie review, and progress tracking in one private workflow.

## Primary Audience

- Mindsparks 26 CodeFront judges evaluating product completeness, technical clarity, and presentation quality.
- First-time users who need clear setup, camera guidance, safety notes, and familiar food examples.
- Real-device testers who need to understand why pose counting pauses or rejects a movement.

## Product Principles

- Count only trustworthy reps. Missed reps are better than false reps.
- Keep live camera work local wherever possible.
- Make the next useful action visible on every route.
- Use Bangla + English copy when it helps comprehension.
- Let the UI feel competition-ready without becoming a landing page.

## Route Goals

- Dashboard: command center for the next workout, demo path, and product loop.
- Pose Coach: camera-first coaching surface with strict readouts and hidden diagnostics.
- Exercises: searchable movement guidance and supported coach entry points.
- Diet Chart: familiar meal planning with clear macro and calorie estimates.
- Calorie Check: low-friction desktop upload or phone QR capture.
- Progress: summaries, recent sessions, and coach diagnostics.
- Demo: official first-time presenter flow and acceptance checklist.
- Onboarding: guided profile setup that unlocks better plans and nutrition.
- About Competition: lightweight submission and competition context.

## Demo Narrative

Start with profile setup, open the dashboard, run Pose Coach, show exercise guidance, generate the diet chart, check a meal photo, close with progress history, then show the submission package.
